**Decisions**

* **Focus Strategy for Q3/Q4 2026:** Decided to focus the remainder of the year on content production, ecosystem education, and strategy preparation to prime the community for the 2027 budget and execution cycle.
* **Expansion of Verticals:** Decided to explore and research Agriculture (under RWA) and Quantum Resistance as new marketing/growth verticals for the Cardano narrative.
* **Adoption of Monthly Public Reporting:** Decided to implement a monthly recap report (to be published as an article and pinned in Discord) to improve public sentiment and transparency ahead of future funding requests.
